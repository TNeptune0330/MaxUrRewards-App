Features

Card Management

  • Natural language input: add cards like “Chase Sapphire Reserve”, “Citi Double Cash”.
  • Billing date tracking to optimize cycle timing.
  • Reward structures with categories, rates, caps, bonuses.
  • Encrypted local database (SQLite) for sensitive data.

Smart Recommendations

  • Location detection via iOS (GPS) + merchant category mapping.
  • Manual override for edge cases (e.g., Walmart → Grocery vs Pharmacy).
  • Real‑time reward comparison across cards.
  • Tie‑breakers consider billing proximity and category caps.

How it works

MaxUrRewards matches your current context to your saved card profiles, computes effective reward rates, and shows a ranked recommendation — all on‑device. Your card list, reward rules, and usage preferences never leave your phone.

  • No bank logins or account linking required.
  • No cloud sync; no third‑party analytics.
  • Location is used only to map merchant category and is not stored or transmitted.

Privacy

Summary: MaxUrRewards is designed to keep your information private. The app stores card names, reward rules, and preferences locally on your device in an encrypted database. We do not operate servers for this app, do not collect analytics, and do not sell or share personal data.

  • Data collected: None sent to us. Data you enter stays on your device.
  • Location: Used in‑app to derive merchant category; not stored or transmitted.
  • Contact: If you email support, we receive the email and any details you provide. We use this only to respond.
